ABOUT THE LAB BOOKING SYSTEM


The Lab Booking system is an online booking facility that facilitates students and lecturers to book for computer labs, it check conflicts and allows simultaneous bookings, cancellation of bookings, and viewing of booking details. The system allows the lab administrators to publish labs, view published schedule, and cancel published labs.
