ABOUT THE LAB BOOKING SYSTEM


The Lab Booking system is an online booking facility that facilitates lab administrators and lecturers to book for computer labs, it check conflicts and allows simultaneous bookings, updates of bookings, changes and deletion of bookings. The system allows the lab administrators to add labs under their names, edit lab details, delete labs. They may also add lab administrators. The system allows calendar view of all the labs, it gives daily, weekly and monthly views of the calendar
